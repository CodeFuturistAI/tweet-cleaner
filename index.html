About

Full control over
your Twitter history

Tweet Cleaner gives you precise filters to delete exactly what you want — and keep what you don't.

🔢

Delete by count

Choose 10, 50, 100, 1000, or delete everything. You decide exactly how many tweets to remove.

📅

Date range filter

Delete only tweets from a specific period. Clean up everything before 2020 while keeping recent posts.

🔍

Keyword filter

Delete tweets that contain a word. Or keep tweets that contain a word. Both directions, full control.

📂

Content types

Choose what to delete: tweets, replies, retweets — any combination. Mix and match freely.

🔎

Scan before delete

The extension scans first and shows you exactly how many tweets match your filters — before deleting anything.

🌍

7 languages

English, Turkish, German, Spanish, French, Portuguese, and Arabic. The UI adapts to your browser language automatically.

Help

Three steps to a
cleaner timeline

No setup, no accounts, no configuration. Just go to your profile and start.

01

Go to your X profile

Open x.com/your_username in Chrome. For replies, go to the Replies tab on your profile. You must be logged in and on your own profile.

02

Open the extension & configure

Click the Tweet Cleaner icon in your Chrome toolbar. Set your filters: how many, which dates, which types, and optional keyword filters.

03

Scan, confirm & delete

Click Start. The extension scans first and shows you the count. Review the results, confirm, and it deletes automatically. Keep the tab open while it runs.

⚠

Back up first

Deletion is permanent. Before starting, go to X Settings → Download your data to keep a backup of your tweets.

Privacy

Zero data collection.
We mean zero.

Your username, password, tweets, and browsing history never leave your device. Not to us, not to anyone.

🔒

No username or password

The extension uses your existing logged-in session on X.com. We never see or store your credentials.

🖥️

All local, all browser

Every operation happens inside your browser tab. No external servers, no data transmission, ever.

📊

No analytics or tracking

We don't use Google Analytics, Mixpanel, or any tracking service. We have no idea how many people use this extension.

💾

Local preferences only

Your filter settings (count, dates, delay) are saved locally with Chrome's storage API. They never leave your device.
