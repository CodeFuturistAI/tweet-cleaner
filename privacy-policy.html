🔒 Privacy Policy

Tweet Cleaner

Last updated: April 25, 2026  ·  Developer: Engin Capat

Short version: Tweet Cleaner does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data whatsoever. Everything happens locally in your browser. No servers. No accounts. No tracking.

1. What data we collect

None. Tweet Cleaner does not collect any personal data, usage data, or any other information from its users.

2. How the extension works

Tweet Cleaner operates entirely within your browser tab on X.com (formerly Twitter). It:

  • Reads tweet content visible on your own profile page to identify tweets matching your filters.
  • Sends delete requests to X.com on your behalf, using your existing logged-in session — exactly as if you clicked "Delete" manually.
  • Stores your filter preferences (count, date range, delay setting) locally in Chrome's storage API — this data never leaves your device.

3. Data we do NOT collect

  • Your X / Twitter username or password
  • Your tweet content
  • Your browsing history
  • Your IP address
  • Any analytics or usage statistics
  • Any personal or identifying information

4. Data storage

Your filter preferences (e.g. how many tweets to delete, date range) are saved locally on your device using Chrome's chrome.storage API. This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to any server.

5. Third-party services

Tweet Cleaner does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The extension does not communicate with any external server other than X.com (which you are already logged into).

6. Permissions

Tweet Cleaner requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage — to save your filter preferences locally on your device.
  • tabs — to detect which tab is open and whether you are on your X.com profile.
  • scripting — to interact with the X.com page to scan and delete tweets.
  • host permission for x.com / twitter.com — required to operate on those pages.

None of these permissions are used to collect or transmit data.

7. Children's privacy

Tweet Cleaner does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to this policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published at the same URL with a revised "Last updated" date.
